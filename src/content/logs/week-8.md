### Lessons Learned
Context-Driven Design: Real-world environments (like a bright cafe counter) must dictate UI choices like contrast and font weight.

Modular Scalability: Building a portfolio as a "system" rather than a static page makes future updates significantly easier.

The Power of Polish: Dedicating entire days to testing and polishing (May 6) ensures that small UX bugs don't escalate into system failures.
